About Dream Tech Liberia

Dream Tech Liberia is dedicated to empowering young people in Liberia by providing hands-on training in web development, Python game programming, and Unity game design. We aim to inspire creativity, innovation, and future tech careers by helping students build websites, develop video games, and explore technology. Our mission is to bridge the digital skills gap, ensuring a brighter future for the next generation in Liberia.

We also offer bootcamp for students at verail high school.
